# Artificial Intelligence (AI)

A sometimes woolly and loaded term, "Artificial Intelligence", is computation that is constructed to mimic human learning, understanding, and autonomy (Stryker and Kavlakoglu, 2024). 

This research addresses [[generative-ai]], a sub-branch of artificial intelligence, which specifically focuses on the creation material, such as original text, images and video.
